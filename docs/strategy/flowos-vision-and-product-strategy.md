## North star

> **Turn intention into execution. Turn execution into progress. Turn reflection into improvement.**

**Core loop:** Direction → Planning → Commitment → Execution → Evidence → Reflection → Adaptation

Or simply: **Plan → Commit → Focus → Reflect → Improve**

FlowOS is a **personal execution and continuous improvement system** — not merely another task manager, calendar, focus timer, habit tracker, or dashboard.
